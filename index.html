With Maksad Ek Kadam

We are a volunteer group from Ranchi with an aim for rural development and child welfare. It's the latent talent of our group which is materializing in the form of work we do. We as a team focus on improving the quality of life and economic well-being of the people of the rural areas. Apart from that safeguarding children from violence, exploitation, abuse, neglect, and providing them with quality education is also before us. We do our best so that insight into the work is remarkable for all who seek us.

Prabhat Khabar

Date: 12th July 2021
The team organized various camps all over the city of Ranchi. We have successfully carried out 12 vaccination drives to this day and vaccinated more than 1200 people. We take great pride in helping society and make India more potent in the fight against COVID-19.
